sample scenes

 

A series of sound scenarios or scenes are included in Diesel Studio. These scenes illustrate the different ways in which 3-D sound operates in practice. Of course, as a user of Diesel Studio, you can, besides just listening to the scenes, also make changes in them and add new files. Before you begin to create scenes yourself with Diesel Studio you ought to experience some of those, which are included. They are constructed so that you can hear the various sound features Diesel Studio can offer.

Circle of numbers
Hear how the numbers 1-16 are placed at different locations around you at intervals of 22.5 degrees.

City Walk
A simple scenario where you walk around in a city. You hear a woman with high-heels walking towards you.

Traffic
This is a complex scene created by using just two, short sound fragments. Diesel Studio offers the possibility of setting a series of parameters, which will give a dynamic expression to otherwise simple, short sounds. You are standing at a cross-roads and cars are driving past you in all directions.
If you wish, you can add more cars to the scene, thereby creating even more complexity.

Meeting Environment
Virtual Meeting Environment is a system where binaural sound is used to improve communication conditions.

In this example the system is implemented to improve the comprehension of four independent speakers. In the first 6 seconds the voices are placed in mono. Subsequently, they are quickly placed in the room in front of the listener.

At the start it will be difficult to separate the voices in order to understand them but after they have been placed individually, it becomes possible to focus on a single sound source.

It is possible to separate 2 sound sources with traditional stereo, while with binaural technology 4 - 6 sound sources can be separated. The advantages of binaural technology, in connection with telephonic conference systems, are obvious.

Pink Floyd Style
Binaural technology has been used in music recordings for many years. This has been done by using a technique called "artificial head recording", where the listener has been placed at a certain location, for example in a symphony orchestra.
With interactive 3-D Audio it is possible to create a totally different music experience, where not only the listener is positioned, but also where the music experience itself can change character.

The Pink Floyd Style is a classic psychedelic rock-ballad. Beside the openness in the soundscape the smooth change in position gives new and surprising figures in the music experience.

Rio
When you hear the different rhythm instruments, you will experience the feeling of dancing in the streets of Rio de Janeiro.

Weird Dream
Placed in a weird world with sounds all around you. In this scenario different sound – e.g. a barking dog - is used as music instruments. The exact position of the sound sources gives a high resolution of the soundscape.

The Big Blue
 Somewhere deep in an ocean, or just above the water, you are in a large space surrounded by humpback whales and water dripping just next to you. In The Big Blue scenario you will experience the huge openness of the audio provided by Diesel Power ä
