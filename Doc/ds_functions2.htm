functions - view windows

 

The Diesel Studio provides four views in the audio world: 

  1. from the top looking down at the xy elevation.
  2. from the side looking at the yz elevation. 
  3. from the back looking at the xz elevation. 
  4. from a perspective looking at the xy elevation.

Note:
It is only possible to edit the path and the objects in the top, side, and back view!
The grids used in the views are centred in 0, 0, 0 and the spacing between the lines is 1 meter.
