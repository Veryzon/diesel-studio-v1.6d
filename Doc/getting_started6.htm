Introducing: Pitch
Pitch is used when changing the speed of an engine, for example, or to change the playback.

How?
1. Go to File
2. Load Tutorial5.dss
3. Play

You will hear a car driving past from left to right, but the sound of the car is not compatible with its speed. You can rectify this by changing pitch.

How?
1. Select 'Car' in the Object Edit box
2. Go to Parameter
3. Drag slider in Pitch to 2.0
4. Play

Now you will be able to hear that speed and sound are compatible.
