getting started - tutorial 3

 

Introducing: Doppler, Line, Velocity

1. Choose sample scene: Tutorial 2.dss
2. Select play

You will now hear an ambulance following an installed "Line Path". It comes towards you, drives past and then away from you. You may think that the ambulance is going too slowly. This can be changed.

How?
1. Select 'Ambu' in the Object Edit box
2. Select Line Path
3. Select both points (click the first item, and then hold down SHIFT and click the other items)
4. Select Distribute time
5. Change 30:00 to 08:00
6. Click OK
7. Select Play

You will now hear the ambulance driving past you at speed. You will also hear the sound phenomenon known as Doppler. The amount of Doppler you want can be adjusted at your sound source.

1. Choose Parameter
2. Drag Slider Doppler to 5
3. Select Play
